ScriptBlock gives you greater control over what javascript, iframes, and plugins runs in your browser to increase security and lower the CPU usage. It is useful to help mitigate attacks like cross-site scripting (XSS) and drive by downloads by minimizing the third party content run.

For more information, updates, and to view the change log, please visit the project homepage at:
https://code.google.com/p/scriptblock/
